About me

“Only you and you alone can change your situation. Don’t blame it on anything or anyone.” Leonardo DiCaprio

When you try doing things perfectly there is no doubt you would end up doing it wrong. I am not saying perfection doesn't exist but what it takes comes from experience. The fear of doing something wrong or taking wrong decisions is what makes us weak.

I have made lots of mistakes in my short span of life and believe me doing mistakes is what makes you experienced and strong. I would rather be a old man regretting my mistakes than being a person who feels sorry for himself wondering what he could have done when he had all that time.

Feel free to take wrong turns, walk those paths less travelled, say what you want. Do what makes you happy now. We don't know what future has for us, instead of being scared of it. I choose to be optimistic and pretend that all is well and will always will.

Hi, I am Sudhir Kumar. A developer by profession living in holy city of Haridwar since last two years. I am learning every day to be a better human being and a good son to my parents. About my job it keeps me busy through the week and on weekends I like travelling, writing and listening music. With that said, if you want to get in touch feel free to reach me out. Love yourself and live your life the way you want.
